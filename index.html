Malelficus :: Malware ELF Tool

Malelficus is a malefic tool to dissect and infect ELF binaries, and it was created to help in process of malware development and analysis of ELF binaries on Linux.

With malELFicus you could modify a ELF binary, infecting a specific segment or append your own segment in the binary!
